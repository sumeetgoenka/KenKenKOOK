Hi, I'm Anay. I'm in Year 9 and I'm preparing for KenKen competitions.

I built this tool to give me basically unlimited KenKens to practice with — so instead of running out of puzzles or doing the same ones twice, I can sit down, dial in the difficulty, grid size, and the operators I want to drill, and immediately get a fresh printable sheet.

Every puzzle the tool produces is solver-verified to have exactly one solution, so practice time is never wasted on a broken puzzle. Print sheets come out as a real PDF with exact millimeter layouts, so they look the same on every printer.

Always solvable

Every puzzle is verified by a backtracking solver to have exactly one solution.

3×3 to 5×5

Pick your grid size and difficulty to match exactly the level you're practicing.

Print-perfect PDF

Layouts of 1, 2, 4, 8, or 16 puzzles per page, drawn at exact mm coordinates.

Drill any operator

Toggle +, −, ×, ÷ to focus on the operations you want to get faster at.
